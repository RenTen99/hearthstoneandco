What Is Per Diem Staffing

Flexible Shift Coverage When Your Facility Needs It Most

Per diem staffing means exactly what it sounds like — a clinician works a shift, as needed, without a long-term commitment from either party. It's the fastest way to fill an open shift without burdening permanent staff with mandatory overtime.

Hearthstone maintains an active Houston-area pool of pre-credentialed per diem clinicians specifically to respond to short-notice and same-day requests. Every clinician in the pool is vetted, background-checked, and credentialed before they ever receive a shift offer.

Same-Week Coverage — How It Works

Facilities with an active Hearthstone staffing agreement can request same-week coverage through a simple process.

  • Submit your open shift — unit, date, time, skill level needed
  • Hearthstone confirms a matched clinician from the active pool
  • Credentialing packet provided before the shift starts
  • Clinician arrives compliant and briefed on facility requirements
  • You receive a timesheet and invoice after shift completion
  • Rate and fill confirmation documented for your records

Per diem LVNs for skilled nursing, long-term care, assisted living, outpatient, and dialysis settings. Medication administration and wound care competency available.

  • Texas LVN license verified, active status
  • SNF, ALF, clinic, and dialysis experience

  • Texas Nurse Aide Registry verified

  • ADL assistance and mobility transfer experience

FAQ

Per Diem Nurse Staffing — Common Questions

How quickly can Hearthstone provide per diem coverage?

For facilities with an active staffing agreement, we can typically confirm shift coverage within hours for clinicians already in our pre-credentialed network. Same-week coverage is standard; same-day is possible for common roles like CNAs and LVNs depending on availability.

Do we need a long-term contract to access per diem staffing?

No. Hearthstone offers per diem coverage under a straightforward staffing services agreement, not a long-term commitment. You request coverage as you need it — there's no minimum usage requirement.

What documentation do we receive for per diem placements?

Prior to each placement, you receive a credentialing summary for the assigned clinician — confirming their licensure status, background check clearance, BLS certification, and immunization records. A signed timesheet and invoice follow after each shift.

Can per diem clinicians work multiple shifts or extended schedules?

Yes. Per diem doesn't mean one shift only. If a clinician is a good fit and you need continued coverage, shifts can be scheduled on an ongoing basis. For extended needs lasting several weeks or months, we may recommend transitioning to a contract staffing agreement for added stability.

Do you serve SNFs and assisted living facilities, not just hospitals?

Absolutely. Hearthstone places per diem clinicians across acute hospitals, skilled nursing facilities (SNFs), assisted living communities, dialysis centers, outpatient clinics, and home health agencies throughout Greater Houston.
