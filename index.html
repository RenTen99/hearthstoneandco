The Process

Staffing That Moves at the Speed of Care

From first contact to a credentialed clinician on-site — our placement process is built for speed without sacrificing quality or compliance.

1

Submit Your Staffing Request

Tell us about your facility's needs — roles, shift types, coverage model, and start date. Our structured intake form captures everything our recruiters need to get started immediately.

2

We Match Pre-Screened Clinicians

Our team reviews your requirements and sources credentialed professionals from our active network. No unvetted candidates — ever. Every match is deliberate and compliant.

3

Compliance Confirmation

License verification, background screening, drug testing, and OIG/SAM checks are completed before any assignment is confirmed. Your compliance is protected at every stage.

4

Placement & Ongoing Support

Your clinician reports for assignment with all documentation in order. A dedicated point of contact remains available throughout the engagement.
