Who we are.

Hearthstone & Co. Workforce Solutions is a healthcare staffing agency headquartered in Houston, Texas, operating as a division of The Shiylohe Collective. We provide per diem, contract, travel, and temp-to-hire staffing to skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, home health agencies, dialysis centers, hospitals, and outpatient clinics throughout Greater Houston.

We operate with a compliance-first model: every professional placed through Hearthstone & Co. undergoes a rigorous pre-placement credentialing process before their first day.

Our agency was built on the premise that healthcare facilities and healthcare professionals both deserve better than the average staffing experience — and that reliability, transparency, and accountability are not optional features.

Mission

To provide Greater Houston healthcare facilities with credentialed, reliable, and compliant workforce solutions that reduce operational risk and support the professionals who deliver care every day.

Vision

To become the most trusted healthcare staffing partner in the Greater Houston metropolitan area by consistently delivering professionals who meet the highest standards of credential integrity and clinical readiness.

Full-TimeHouston, TXHybrid
Role Overview

The Healthcare Recruiter is responsible for sourcing, screening, credentialing, and placing qualified healthcare professionals — CNAs, LVNs, RNs, and allied health staff — with our facility client network across Greater Houston.

High activity, organized follow-through, and clear communication with both clinicians and facility contacts are the core requirements.

Responsibilities
—Source and screen CNA, LVN, RN, MA, and PCT candidates via job boards, referrals, and direct outreach
—Manage credential file completion: license, background, drug screen, immunization, CPR/BLS
—Match professionals to facility requests based on availability and credential requirements
—Maintain ongoing communication with active clinicians to ensure availability and fill rate targets
—Document all activity in the ATS/CRM accurately and on time
Requirements
✓1–3 years recruiting experience; healthcare staffing preferred
✓Familiarity with TX BON verification, background checks, drug screening
✓High-volume activity tolerance — this is a phone-and-pipeline role
✓Houston, TX — in-person or hybrid availability required

Payroll & Billing Specialist
Full-TimeHouston, TXHybrid
Role Overview

The Payroll & Billing Specialist manages the complete financial operations cycle — from timesheet collection and approval through clinician payroll processing and facility invoice generation.

Accuracy and timeliness are the core requirements. Clinicians depend on on-time pay. Facility clients depend on accurate invoicing. This is a mission-critical function.

Responsibilities
—Collect, verify, and process weekly timesheets from all active clinicians
—Run weekly payroll cycles in compliance with Texas labor requirements
—Generate and deliver facility invoices; track AR and follow up on outstanding balances
—Reconcile payroll and billing records; identify and resolve discrepancies promptly
—Maintain audit-ready financial documentation for all transactions
Requirements
✓2+ years payroll processing or billing — staffing or healthcare preferred
✓Proficiency in payroll software (QuickBooks, Paychex, ADP, or equivalent)
✓Detail-oriented with strong reconciliation and documentation discipline
✓Understanding of Texas labor law, overtime, and classification rules
✓Houston, TX — in-person or hybrid availability required
Credentialing Coordinator
Full-TimeHouston, TXHybrid
Role Overview

The Credentialing Coordinator owns the compliance pipeline for every healthcare professional in our network. You are the last checkpoint before a professional reaches a facility.

This role requires precision, follow-through, and the ability to manage multiple candidate files simultaneously without letting anything slip through.

Responsibilities
—Manage the complete pre-placement credentialing process for all incoming clinicians
—Verify active licenses through Texas BON and applicable credentialing bodies
—Coordinate background screening, drug testing, immunization review, and CPR/BLS verification
—Run OIG and SAM exclusion checks; maintain screening documentation
—Track expiration dates; initiate renewals proactively
—Prepare audit-ready credential files and respond to facility compliance requests
Requirements
✓1–3 years in healthcare credentialing, compliance, or medical staffing operations
✓Working knowledge of TX BON verification, OIG/SAM screening, and HIPAA
✓Exceptional attention to detail — no tolerance for incomplete files
✓Ability to manage 20+ concurrent credential files without errors
