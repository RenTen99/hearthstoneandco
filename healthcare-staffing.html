Healthcare Staffing Solutions Built for Modern Care Environments

Hearthstone & Co. delivers credentialed, compliant healthcare professionals through four flexible staffing models designed for operational reliability.

Our Services

Four Staffing Models. One Standard of Quality.

Per Diem Staffing

Per Diem

Last-minute gaps, census fluctuations, and unplanned absence coverage. Our per diem network of pre-credentialed clinicians is maintained specifically for short-notice deployment.

  • Single shifts through extended coverage
  • All shifts: days, nights, weekends, holidays
  • Coverage options within hours of request

Contract Staffing

Contract

Structured 4–26 week assignments for known gaps, leave coverage, census growth, or new program launches. Stability for your facility and consistent engagement for clinicians.

  • 4–26 week structured agreements
  • Role-specific matching and credential review
  • Scalable to multi-role contract programs

Travel Clinicians

Travel

Credentialed travel professionals for specialized, hard-to-fill, or high-acuity roles. Hearthstone manages credentialing, compliance documentation, and onboarding coordination.

  • RNs, allied health, and specialty clinicians
  • Full credentialing managed by Hearthstone
  • 13-week standard with extension options

Temp-to-Hire

Temp-to-Hire

Evaluate a clinician's performance and cultural fit in your environment before extending a permanent offer. Reduces hiring risk and cost.

  • Evaluate before committing to permanent hire
  • Reduce turnover risk and onboarding cost
  • Seamless transition to direct employment
