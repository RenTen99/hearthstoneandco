Our Pillars

The Principles Behind Every Placement

🏠

Local Market Expertise

We operate exclusively in Greater Houston. We know the facilities, patient populations, regulatory environment, and talent pool — advantages national agencies cannot replicate remotely.

⚡

Speed Without Compromise

Our 48-hour average turnaround is maintained by keeping an active, credentialed network ready to deploy. Speed never means skipping compliance — it means our process is efficient.

✓

Rigorous Quality Standards

Every clinician completes license verification, background screening, drug testing, OIG/SAM checks, and role validation. Facilities receive verified candidates — not forwarded resumes.

👥

Partnership-Oriented Service

We assign a named point of contact to every facility and clinician. When your scheduler calls at 6am with an urgent gap, someone answers. That is a standard we hold ourselves to.

📋

Flexible Staffing Models

Per diem, contract, travel, and temp-to-hire — the engagement is structured to fit your operational reality. Hearthstone adapts as your needs evolve.

📈

Scalable Workforce Support

Whether you need two CNAs for weekend coverage or a multi-role contract program, our team scales to support your volume without losing quality or responsiveness.
