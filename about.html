About Hearthstone & Co. Workforce Solutions

A Houston-based healthcare workforce solutions company built on the belief that facilities deserve a staffing partner — not just a staffing vendor.

Our Story

Built for the Facility That Expects More

Hearthstone & Co. Workforce Solutions was founded with a clear purpose: to give healthcare organizations across Greater Houston a staffing partner that operates with the professionalism, urgency, and accountability that patient care demands.

We recognized a persistent gap in the market — facilities dealing with slow response times, inconsistent quality, and agencies that treated every placement as a transaction rather than a professional responsibility. Hearthstone was built to close that gap.

As a subsidiary of The Shiylohe Collective, a veteran-owned parent company, we carry a deep commitment to operational discipline, integrity in execution, and service that extends beyond the placement.

Our Mission

To connect healthcare organizations with dependable, credentialed professionals who strengthen care delivery — with the speed, compliance, and professionalism modern care environments require.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted workforce solutions partner for healthcare organizations across Texas and beyond — known for reliability, quality, and the people behind every placement.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Hearthstone & Co. was founded by Renaye Mullings — a U.S. Army veteran and IT professional with deep experience in operational systems and service organizations. Built on the conviction that Houston healthcare facilities deserve a staffing partner — not a transactional vendor — the company reflects a commitment to compliance, responsiveness, and genuine partnership.
